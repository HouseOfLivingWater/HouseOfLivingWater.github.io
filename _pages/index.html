House of Living Water is a holistic wellness store that addresses the epidemic of dehydration in our community and beyond.

Co-owners Paulina and Cory Milan

Young entrepreneurs passionate about making a difference through collaboration and teamwork.

Our goal is to create a ripple effect of better health and greater wealth, helping others embrace a natural, clean approach to true healing.

We believe that health and wealth are interconnected. In today’s world, finding daily peace often depends on reducing stress—something we aim to address by fostering both physical and financial well-being.

At House of Living Water, we are dedicated to building generational wealth and encouraging the next generation to thrive by promoting sustainable, holistic living.

The products we offer

Star Product: Hydrogen-Rich Water

Our flagship offering is hydrogen-rich water, made possible by the cutting-edge Enagic Kangen Water technology. This product ensures clean and sustainable hydration, meeting the community’s demand for higher-quality water.

Focus on Education and Sustainability

Beyond providing hydration, we strive to educate the community about the importance of reducing waste and embracing sustainable practices.

Future Expansion Plan

In the summer of 2025, we aim to introduce a refill station at House of Living Water, offering an innovative solution for environmentally conscious customers.

Bulk Product Offerings

The refill station will feature a range of home and health essentials—like shampoo, conditioner, body wash, laundry detergent, toothpaste, and dish soap—available for purchase in bulk using reusable containers.

Commitment to Reducing Waste

This expansion underscores our dedication to sustainability by minimizing single-use plastic waste and encouraging eco-friendly practices within the community.
